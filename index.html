SASH

Keeping the culture alive and growing

SASH means a simple drape or a piece of clothing enhancing the beauty of oneself giving it a royal touch . We here at SASH keep launching online workshops as well as online/offline dance classes . We are open for corporate events and paid choreography for various functions . Our aim is to spread happiness through our art of dancing . We have successfully implemented 3 workshops so far and have organized a few events and the list goes on .
